## What you get

### Branded digital floor
A web and mobile-optimised marketplace **carrying your name, your colours, your logo, and your auction house's identity**. Buyers and sellers see [auction house name] — not us.

### Live throughout the sale week
Listings stay open 24/7 instead of only during the Saturday physical sale. Sellers can list a Holstein heifer on Tuesday and have ten bids on it by Friday morning, in time for the floor.

### Integrated with Paynow for payment
Every sale settles through your existing Paynow merchant account. Cash-handling friction drops. Float and security risk drop with it. You see every settlement in the same Paynow dashboard you already use.

### Bisafe escrow for buyer trust
Funds held by Paynow's escrow product until the buyer collects. The US$1,000 cash deposit currently filtering serious bidders gets replaced by a small refundable digital hold — buyers who can't currently afford to walk in with cash become buyers.

### Auction-won and auction-sold SMS notifications
Both sides of every trade get an SMS within seconds of close. Buyer gets a Paynow USSD prompt for payment. Seller gets confirmation their animal is sold and payment is on the way.

### Constable workflow tool
A simple interface for your on-site constable to log animal brand verification against each digital listing — turning the existing police-clearance step into a chain-of-custody record you can show inspectors.

### Branded buyer list, post-sale
Every buyer who registers stays in your customer database — yours to remarket to for next month's sale, the new arrivals, the off-season special.

## What the platform handles for you

This is the part that makes us different from a software vendor:

- **We monitor the platform 24/7.** If something breaks at midnight before a Saturday sale, our team is paged, not yours.
- **We handle buyer and seller support.** Confused EcoCash prompt? Refund request? Forgot password? Our number, not yours.
- **We reconcile every payment** against your Paynow dashboard. End-of-month, you get one report — settled total, transaction surcharge, net to you.
- **We tune the platform** based on what we see in your sale data. Bidding window too short? Too few SMS? We adjust.
- **We do a quarterly business review** with you and your bookkeeper. What worked, what's next, what we're seeing on other floors that you'd want to know about.
